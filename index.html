Healthcare Coverage vs. Poverty

Healthcare Coverage vs. Poverty level

Analysis



The purpose of this project is to analyze the poverty status versus the healthcare status of coverage by each of US state. The data was obtained from two sources: the US Census Bureau Fact Finder page a Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System.
Once the data was installed in a excel.csv format, it was accessed in d3. The data is included below.

Based on the data plotted we can conclude:
  • There exists a correlation between poverty status and population with poor healthcare coverage. States as Missisipi that poverty of 21.5% and lacks healthcare coverage of 18.8% versus states as Massachusets with a poverty level of 11.6% and lacks healthcare coverage of 4.6%.
